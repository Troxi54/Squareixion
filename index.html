Light

Light resets everything before, but gives light

allows you to hold squares to damage them.

Master

Master resets everything before, but
increases master level to unlock new abilities.
First master level unlocks master milestones.

Damage: 0

Upgrades

Gigalize

Gigalize resets giant square
features, but gives giga squares
to buy good upgrades.

Upgrades

Collapse

Collapse resets everything before
and gives stars to buy powerful upgrades
First collapse also unlocks new realm.

Upgrades

The Strange Place

Entering the strange place forces collapse. All pre-collapse currency gain0.5. Leaving the strange place gives you black holes based on your stage.

Rebuild

Rebuild resets everything before, but gives universe generators for rebuild features.
First rebuild unlocks new realm.

The Unique Place

Entering the unique place place forces rebuild. All pre-rebuild currency's overflow starts immediately and you can't increase your master level. Leaving the unique place gives you white holes based on your stage starting at 100.
